Doug started Sure-Dry in 1994 and oversees the leadership team. The company has helped over 25,000 homeowners since then! Doug's beliefs and energy is always people focused and driven. He is a mentor to many family, friends and other business owners. Doug's other motivator - restoring (and collecting) antique Allis-Chalmers tractors. You can see a few of them during the summer months at tractor shows and farm markets.

At Sure-Dry we work every day to raise and set the industry standards and provide a remarkable experience for each of our customers. Doug's conviction and leadership is at the very core of delivering on that mission. 

"Our teams go above and beyond to deliver on every customer’s goals and proudly stand behind their work,” says Doug.

We are excited to have Doug as part of the Sure-Dry team as we work to redefine the industry and create positive, remarkable experiences.
