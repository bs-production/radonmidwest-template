While home radon is dangerous, other indoor air pollutants can be just as much of a problem. If your home has indoor air quality concerns, we offer a variety of solutions that we can customize to your property, including:

  • Energy recovery ventilator: An ERV replaces stale indoor air with fresh outdoor air - this also gives indoor air pollutants a way to escape the home.
  • Vapor intrusion mitigation: A vapor intrusion mitigation system prevents VOCs in the soil beneath the home from entering the property.
  • Breathe EZ Air Cleaner: The Breathe EZ Air Cleaner electronically charges air pollutants in the HVAC system, causing them to cling to the filter media. With carbon strips, the system also absorbs foul odors and hazardous chemicals.
  • Breathe EZ UVC Light: The Breathe EZ UVC Light kills mold and other bacteria that commonly grows inside the HVAC system.
  • Dehumidifiers: Dark, wet, and humid spaces are typical areas for mold growth. That is why we commonly install dehumidifiers in the basement or crawl space.
